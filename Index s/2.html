About Us

TFP's Curry Powders is a venture by 3 housewives-Ancy Thomas,Mercy Thomas and Salamma Sebastian to provide pure home made spice powders. Started in 2015, TFP's is growing in a fast pace providing pure spice powders in the near localities. Apart from spice powders they are supplying rice powders like puttu podi, appam podi, idiyappam podi and snacks like unniyappam,neyyappam, avalosunda, achappam etc as well.

TFP's is where you can find best in curry powders, organic curry masalas, and other custom seasoning blends with the highest level of quality. Indulge your taste buds, even professional cooks can enjoy some new and interesting tastes.I hope you enjoy these dishes as much as we do and any ideas or suggestions are most welcome.


Happy cooking.
