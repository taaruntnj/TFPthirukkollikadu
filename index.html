About Us

Rita's Curry Powders is a venture by 3 housewives-Ancy Thomas,Mercy Thomas and Salamma Sebastian to provide pure home made spice powders. Started in 2015, Rita's is growing in a fast pace providing pure spice powders in the near localities. Apart from spice powders they are supplying rice powders like puttu podi, appam podi, idiyappam podi and snacks like unniyappam,neyyappam, avalosunda, achappam etc as well.

Rita's is where you can find best in curry powders, organic curry masalas, and other custom seasoning blends with the highest level of quality. Indulge your taste buds, even professional cooks can enjoy some new and interesting tastes.I hope you enjoy these dishes as much as we do and any ideas or suggestions are most welcome.


Happy cooking.

Services

Spice Powders

We provide packed powders of all spices like turmeric powder, coriander powder, chilly powder, kashmiri chilli powder and meat masala, chicken masala and gram masalas as well.

Snacks

We also provide snacks like unniyappam,neyyappam, avalosunda, achappam etc

Pickles

We also have a variety of pickles-manga achar, naranga achar,nellika achar, erachi achar, meen achar etc..

Cutlets

We fill your tastebuds by giving a variety of cutlets including veg, chicken and beef cutlets..

Flours

We also provide a variety of flours like rice flour, wheat flour. We are trending in providing soft puttu podi, idiyappam podi, appam podi, avalosupodi etc..

Pulverising

Apart from all, we provide on demand pulverising of rice, wheat, and all spices in front of you...

Team Rita's

Rita's is run by a 3 women army. They form a dedicated strong team spending 24/7 for Rita's, who are women turned entrepreneurs. Their hardwork is behind the variety of products Rita's has now.
