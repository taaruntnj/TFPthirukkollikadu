Thanjai Foood Products

Thanjai is famous for its delicious South Indian food. You can find popular dishes like Idlis, Dosa, and Vada. These dishes are made with fresh ingredients like coconut, curry leaves, and spices. Thanjai's food is known for its authentic taste and flavor. Whether it's the sweet Payasam or the spicy Sambar, every dish is a treat for your taste. Thanjai's food reflects the region's rich culture and love for good food.

About Dosa

Dosa is a popular South Indian, thin, savory crepe made from a fermented batter of rice and lentils (urad dal), often served with chutneys and sambar. It originated in southern India and can be enjoyed as a breakfast item, snack, or a light meal. Traditional dosas are crispy and served with a spicy potato filling, known as a masala dosa. .
